Data center Ready-for-Service is not a checkbox. It is a confidence calculation.

After working with commissioning teams across enterprise, colocation, and hyperscale facilities, one pattern kept appearing: the gap between "we think we are ready" and "the evidence proves we are ready" is where delays hide.

A single unresolved critical defect at G4 (Functional Performance Tests) can cascade into 3-6 weeks of delay at G5 (Integrated Systems Tests) and G6 (Operational Readiness). But most teams track this in spreadsheets that do not model blocker cascades or evidence dependencies.

So I built the RFS Readiness Workbench -- a gate-driven commissioning tracker that runs entirely in the browser.

What it does differently:

Gate board matrix (G0-G7) that evaluates readiness per delivery unit with hard blocker logic. A critical defect or missing mandatory evidence physically prevents gate progression.

7-term confidence formula that penalizes open critical defects (18 points each), major defects (8 points), missing evidence (7 points), missing approvals (6 points), pending retests (5 points), plus overdue aging and unconfirmed overlay penalties.

Drag-based forecast engine that calculates delay drivers per unit: blocker drag, evidence drag, signoff drag, retest drag, witness drag, and uncertainty drag. The project RFS date is the critical path (worst unit).

Source confidence tagging (A/B/C/D) where only contractual (A) and industry standard (B) requirements can create automatic hard blockers. Benchmark-sourced (C) and internal (D) overlay requirements cannot auto-block gates.

6 facility archetypes (enterprise, retail colo, wholesale hyperscale, hyperscale self-build, AI fast-track, regulated/sovereign) with baseline calibration multipliers and risk tag profiles.

99 commissioning requirements mapped across all 8 gates covering electrical, mechanical, fire, controls, security, operations, regulatory, and commercial disciplines.

No backend. No vendor lock-in. Client-side localStorage with JSON export/import. Free tier covers the core workflow. Pro unlocks test packages, customer overlay, advanced forecast, and full reporting.
