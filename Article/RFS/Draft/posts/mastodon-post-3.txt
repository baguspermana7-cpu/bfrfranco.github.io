Hyperscale customer overlays are a top source of RFS delay. A "customer-specific" requirement can block turnover if source confidence is low.

The RFS Workbench uses source class tagging (A-D). Only A/B sources can auto-block gates. C/D gets a "Benchmark only" badge.

This distinction matters. Over-engineering from benchmarks wastes as much time as under-engineering from contracts.
