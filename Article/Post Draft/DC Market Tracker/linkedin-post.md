The data reveals some patterns worth noting:

13 markets now exceed 1 GW of operational capacity. Northern Virginia leads at 4,040 MW with another 5,500 MW in the planning pipeline. That single market has more capacity than most countries.

The fastest growth is not where most people expect. Dubai, Nairobi, and Kuala Lumpur are growing at 20-25% CAGR. Established markets like Amsterdam and Dublin are growing at single digits. The next billion-dollar build-outs are in APAC, Middle East, and Africa.
