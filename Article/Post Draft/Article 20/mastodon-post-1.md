# Mastodon Post 1 — Detailed Fact-Check (500 chars max)

Sam Altman told an audience in India that AI water concerns are "mostly fake."

As a data center engineer with 12+ years experience, I fact-checked every claim:

- U.S. data centers used 17 billion gallons in 2023
- Projected to hit 68 billion by 2028 (4x increase)
- 56% of facilities still use evaporative cooling
- Microsoft's own water usage rose 34% in one year
- Google takes 29% of one Oregon city's water

Some viral stats ARE misleading. But the trend is real.
