When you optimize a factory, you optimize the entire system — not just one machine. That means CPU, GPU, NVLink, NIC, switch ASIC, software, storage, AND the optical interconnect that ties it all together.

$4B in photonics is not a bet on fiber optics. It's a bet on system-level optimization.
