# Mastodon Post 3 — Diversity Gap (500 chars max)

The data center workforce has a serious diversity problem:

- 50% of facilities have less than 5% women on staff
- Average engineer age: ~60 years old
- 32% of workers are over 60, only 16% under 30
- 23,000 retire annually

This isn't sustainable. The industry needs a pipeline, not just individual hires. Community colleges, apprenticeships, and targeted recruitment — not just LinkedIn posts.
