Under 5% of data centers have rack densities that actually justify two-phase PFAS cooling (>100 kW/rack). The rest adopted it because competitors did.

Now they're sitting on a problem they haven't priced.

Mid-life cooling swap: 30-45% of original capex. PFAS disposal: $8-15/liter. A 100,000-liter system = $1M liability — on no balance sheet.

3M exited PFAS manufacturing end of 2025. The industry runs on stockpiled supply while pretending this is resolved.
