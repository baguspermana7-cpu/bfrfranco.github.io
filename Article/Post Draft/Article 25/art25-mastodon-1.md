# Mastodon Post 1 — Overview (500 chars max)

The largest power grid in North America is running out of capacity.

PJM Interconnection serves 65 million people across 13 states. By 2027, it faces a projected 6 GW shortfall. Data centers account for 40% of load growth. 40 GW of coal and gas plants are retiring. Capacity auction prices spiked 800% in one year.

In December 2022, PJM lost 46 GW during Winter Storm Elliott. Rolling blackouts were minutes away.
