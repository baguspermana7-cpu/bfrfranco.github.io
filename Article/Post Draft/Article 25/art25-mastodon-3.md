# Mastodon Post 3 — Consumer Impact (500 chars max)

Who pays when data centers consume 40% of grid growth?

PJM capacity auction costs jumped from $2.2B to $14.7B in one year. That translates to $2-5/month more per residential customer.

Meanwhile, Dominion Energy has 30+ GW of DC requests queued — 1.5x its entire system peak. Loudoun County residents are questioning DC tax incentives.

The fundamental tension: everyone wants reliable power, nobody wants to pay for it.
