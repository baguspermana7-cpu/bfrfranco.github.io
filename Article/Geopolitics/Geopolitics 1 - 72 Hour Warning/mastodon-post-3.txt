The 72-hour survival checklist most people miss:

Water: 1 gallon per person per day (3 gal minimum)
Food: 2,000 cal/day non-perishable per person
Medications: 7-day supply of all prescriptions
Power: Battery bank, hand-crank radio
Cash: ATMs and card readers need electricity
Documents: Copies of IDs, insurance, contacts
