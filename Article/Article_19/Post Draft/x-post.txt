Thread: Singapore vs Batam for data centers — what actually matters

1/ Singapore and Batam are 20 km apart. Batam is 2-3x cheaper on electricity, 40-100x on land, 4-5x on labor. Yet most hosting companies still choose Singapore. Here is why — and when they shouldn't.

2/ Singapore has 26+ submarine cables, 260+ peering members at SGIX, and every major cloud provider's primary SEA region. For latency-sensitive workloads, this ecosystem density is worth the premium.

3/ Batam wins on cost-sensitive, latency-tolerant workloads: AI training, batch processing, backup/DR, storage. The operators building there are hyperscale/wholesale — not VPS providers.

4/ The emerging Singapore-Batam Digital Corridor puts front-end in Singapore and back-end in Batam. A dedicated 24-fiber submarine cable (targeting Q4 2026) makes this architecture real.
