# LinkedIn Post — Cx Calculator Launch

Just released a free Data Center Commissioning Calculator covering the full L0-L6 lifecycle.

Most commissioning tools focus on L1-L5 — factory witness testing through integrated systems testing. But two critical phases are often overlooked:

L0 — Design & Cx Planning
Where the OPR, Basis of Design, Cx plan, points list, testability review, and energization strategy set the foundation. A poorly planned commissioning program costs more to fix during execution than it does to get right upfront.

L6 — Turnover & Closeout
Where punch list resolution, residual risk acceptance, operations training, spare parts confirmation, systems manuals, and formal sign-off determine what the customer actually receives.

The tool supports:
- 30 global regions with localized labor rates
- 8 archetype presets: enterprise, colocation, hyperscale, AI factory (100MW+), edge, modular, fast-track repurposed, and recommissioning
- Cooling differentiation: air, in-row, RDHX, DLC, and immersion
- Redundancy from N through 2N+1
- Interactive Gantt chart with 5 levels of expandable depth
- Monte Carlo simulation (10,000 iterations) and sensitivity analysis
- PDF export with full methodology notes

The AI factory preset reflects 2026 reality: 50-100+ kW racks, DLC with CDU redundancy testing, liquid loop zoning verification, rack-level thermal acceptance, and campus-scale phased turnover — informed by public disclosures from programs like Stargate, xAI Colossus, AWS Project Rainier, Google's 1MW rack architecture, and Microsoft's AI Superfactory.

Source confidence is clearly labeled. ASHRAE Guideline 0-2019 provides the lifecycle process backbone, while L0-L6 are project-delivery levels, not ASHRAE-defined hierarchy. Base costs combine BSRIA, Uptime Institute surveys, and NETA standards with expert assumptions.

Free to use, all client-side, no data transmitted.
