Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my qualifications and services

Is ResistanceZero affiliated with any company?
No. ResistanceZero is an independent personal project created out of a genuine interest in data center infrastructure. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representative of any current or former employer. All content is produced in the author's personal time as a self-directed educational hobby.
Where does the data and analysis come from?
All content is derived exclusively from publicly available sources — published industry reports (Synergy, CBRE, JLL), SEC filings, press releases, trade publications (DCD, Uptime Institute), government data, and academic research. No confidential, proprietary, or non-public information from any employer, client, or third party is used. See our Terms of Service Section 6 for full details.
Is this a commercial business or consulting service?
No. ResistanceZero is a personal educational project — it does not offer consulting, advisory, bid support, feasibility studies, or any commercial services. The calculators and articles are free analytical tools built from the author's independent reading and research into publicly available data center methodologies.
Is there a demo account to try the calculator features?
Yes — you can explore all calculator features using the demo account: demo@resistancezero.com / demo2026. This includes advanced KPIs, scenario modeling, PDF executive reports, and more across all calculators and article tools.
What is the CAPEX Calculator?
Estimates data center construction costs - building, electrical (MV/LV), UPS, generators, cooling, fire suppression with seismic zone and redundancy factors (N to 2N+1).
What is the OPEX Calculator?
Estimates annual operational costs - staffing (in-house vs contractor), energy based on PUE, maintenance, insurance. Supports 30+ countries with localized rates.
What is the AI/HPC Telemetry Dashboard?
Demo dashboard for AI/HPC data centers - GPU telemetry (GB200 NVL72), liquid cooling metrics, power tracking, environmental monitoring.
What is the Conventional DC Telemetry?
Demo dashboard for traditional data centers - EPMS monitoring, chiller plant metrics, data hall environment, fire system status, fuel system tracking.
What certifications do you hold?
CDFOM, HV & LV Senior Authorized Person (AP/SAP), Level 6 SKTTK (ESDM), IOSH Managing Safely, Ahli K3 Spesialis Bidang Listrik (AK3L) - Kemnaker RI.
What is CDFOM?
Certified Data Centre Facilities Operations Manager - international certification for managing data center operations, maintenance, and compliance.
What is Ahli K3 Listrik (AK3L)?
Indonesian Electrical Safety Expert certification from Kementerian Ketenagakerjaan - qualifies holder for electrical safety audits and risk assessments.
What is Senior Authorized Person (SAP)?
Highest level of electrical authorization for HV/LV systems - permits holder to authorize switching, isolation, PTW, and supervise high-risk electrical work.
What experience do you have?
12+ years professional experience spanning industrial and critical facilities — with background across hyperscale, colocation, and industrial engineering environments. All content on this site is independent personal research and does not represent any employer.
What systems do you manage?
Electrical (MV/LV, UPS, Generators), Cooling (Chiller, CRAH, CRAC), Fire Detection & Suppression, BMS/DCIM, and critical infrastructure operations.
