Free Float Methodology

This page translates the researched methodology into a local prototype document: strategic holder categories, conditional individual logic, and explicit limitations.

Core Formula

Free float represents shares realistically available to trade. Strategic ownership is removed from the float pool.

Free Float = 100% - Strategic Holdings Strategic Holdings = all non-free-float investor categories plus verified affiliated individuals

Individual Holder Logic

Individual shareholders require an extra reasoning layer. Percentage size alone is not enough.

Is the individual holding 20% or more?
YES -> Strategic

Verified as founder, director, commissioner, family vehicle, or affiliated insider?
YES -> Strategic

Holds material stakes across multiple issuers without control evidence?
YES -> Manual review, not automatic strategic

No control evidence found?
YES -> Free float

      In production, this methodology should be versioned. Every free-float snapshot should store a `method_version` value so future rule changes do not silently rewrite historical results.
