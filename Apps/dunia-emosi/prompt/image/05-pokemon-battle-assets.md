## AI GENERATION TIPS

- Reference: Pokemon Sword/Shield or Scarlet/Violet battle arena screenshots
- Key elements: Floating platform, dramatic sky, energy field
- Colors: Dark blue/purple palette with gold/yellow accents

### For Science Quiz:
- Reference: Pixar's "Lightyear" space scenes + educational game UIs
- Key elements: Space/science equipment floating in dark space
- Colors: Navy, teal, gold particle effects

### For Shadow Game:
- Reference: Shadow Puppet theatre aesthetic + Disney's "Peter Pan" shadow scenes
- Key elements: Theatrical spotlight, deep purple atmosphere
- Colors: Deep purple/navy, white spotlight, gold sparkles

### Style consistency across all new assets:
- AAA game quality (not flat design)
- Dark-themed to match the app's dark AAA UI
- Neon/glow accents in purple/gold/teal
- Child-friendly (not scary, not too realistic)
- PNG transparent where specified
