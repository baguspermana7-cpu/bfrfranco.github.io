## STYLE CONSISTENCY NOTES

- Vehicles must feel like characters with personality
- Obstacles should be clearly threatening but cartoonish — not scary for ages 5+
- All assets must read clearly at game size (120×200px vehicles, 80×80px obstacles)
- CGI assets: volumetric lighting, material sheen, subsurface paint quality
- Anime assets: thick black outlines, vibrant flat cel-fills, motion energy
