## STYLE CONSISTENCY NOTES

- Vehicles must feel like characters with personality (Pixar Cars principle)
- Obstacles should be clearly threatening but cartoonish — not scary for ages 5+
- All assets must read clearly at game size (120×200px vehicles, 80×80px obstacles)
- Pixar assets: volumetric lighting, material sheen, subsurface skin/paint quality
- One Piece assets: thick black outlines `#2D1B00`, vibrant flat cel-fills, motion energy
